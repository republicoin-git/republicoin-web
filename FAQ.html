Frequently Asked Questions

If you are visiting our website (Republicoin.net) for the first time and don’t have a Personal Account, follow these steps:
1. Click the ‘Sign-up’ button in the upper-right
2. Enter your email address, first name, last name, username, email, password, confirm password, and set a password for your account.
3. Review the user agreement and AML & KYC policy.
4. Click the ‘Create an account’ button.
5. After successful registration, log in using your email address and password.

2. Click on the “Log in” button in the upper-right corner.
3. Enter your email address and password.
4. Click “Sign in” to access your Personal Account.

1. On the account login page, click on “Forgot your password.”
2. Enter the email address associated with your account and click “Email Password Reset Link.”
3. Enter the verification code sent to your email address and click “Confirm.”
4. Create a new password for your account and click “Set a password.”

1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Profile” tab and click on “Settings” in the “Account Settings” section.
3. Click on “Change Password.”
4. Enter the verification code sent to your email.
5. Enter your new password twice and click “Change.”

To enable or disable authentication via text message (SMS), follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Profile” tab and click on “Settings” in the “Account Settings” section.
3. Locate the option to enable or disable authentication via SMS.
4. Follow the prompts to complete the process.

The ‘Whitelist’ function allows you to limit the list of wallets to which you can withdraw funds, providing an extra layer of security. To enable the ‘Whitelist’ option, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Profile” tab and select “Address Whitelist.”
3. Click on “Add Withdrawal Address.”
4. Enter the required details for the wallet you want to add.
5. Enter the verification code sent to your email.
6. Save the added wallet address for future withdrawals.

To deposit stablecoins to your wallet, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Funds” section and select “Deposit.”
3. Choose the stablecoin wallet you want to deposit.
4. Select the transfer network that corresponds to the platform you are withdrawing from.
5. Copy the deposit address and use it to initiate the transfer from the corresponding platform.

To deposit fiat money to your wallet, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Funds” section and select “Deposit.”
3. Choose the currency you want to deposit.
4. Select the payment system through which you want to deposit.
5. Specify the amount and follow the instructions of the payment system.

To deposit cryptocurrency to your wallet, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Funds” section and select “Deposit.”
3. Choose the cryptocurrency wallet you want to deposit.
4. Select the cryptocurrency you want to deposit.
5. Copy the deposit code or scan the QR code.
6. Use the provided code to transfer funds from your external wallet.

To withdraw fiat money from your Republicoin wallet, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Funds” section and select “Withdraw.”
3. Choose the currency you want to withdraw.
4. Select the payment system you prefer for withdrawal.
5. Specify the amount and provide the necessary details.
6. Confirm the withdrawal request by entering the verification code sent to your email.

To withdraw Republicoin from your wallet, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your Republicoin account.
2. Go to the “Funds” section and select “Withdraw.”
3. Choose the stablecoin wallet you want to withdraw from.
4. Specify the transfer amount, wallet address, and click “Proceed.”
5. Enter the verification code sent to your email and click “Confirm.”
