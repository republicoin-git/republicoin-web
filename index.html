About Project

RPC Token Overview

The RPC token is a TON and smart contract token on the Ethereum chain. It also serves as the native token on the RPC chain. The total supply of RPC is 800 million, and through continuous burning, the supply will eventually stabilize at 500 million. The RPC token acts as gas for the foundational layer of the RPC ecosystem, which encompasses collaborations with the Republicoin Exchange, as well as applications in payment, entertainment, finance, and other use cases.

Future Development

The future development of the RPC ecosystem is centered around enhancing the core functionalities of RPC and its surrounding infrastructure. This includes creating a fundamental toolchain for the blockchain, integrating centralized and decentralized elements, establishing a global open developer platform, forming a decentralized autonomous community, developing cross-chain interoperability protocols, and expanding the system's performance and capacity to accommodate larger scales and increased efficiency.

Benefits

The RPC ecosystem seeks to foster decentralization and build a community of shared interests that benefits both users and protocols. It envisions an internet of the future that is user-created, user-owned, user-controlled, and protocol-distributed. The benefits include decentralized identity management, NFT ownership rights and transactions, the construction of a metaverse network platform, and initiatives for Layer 2 and GameFi projects.

Autonomus Phase

In the later stages, when the foundations of the Republicoin ecosystem have been laid, we will gradually try to establish more efficient, autonomous, and community-based protocols and tools, and gradually remove power from the Republicoin Management Foundation, allowing users and contributors of the Republicoin ecosystem to decide the future development of the Republicoin ecosystem, enhancing the management weight of the Republicoin community, until management is completely replaced by the Republicoin community. We are convinced that only governance drawn from users can truly serve users.

Republicoin Token (RPC) Description

The Republicoin Token (RPC) was originally issued as a TON token on the Ethereum chain and has since migrated to the RPC chain. The total circulation is 500 million, with token burning measures designed to reduce the supply to 500 million in the future. RPC is the native token of the RPC ecosystem and is expected to gain greater value as the ecosystem expands.

Use of RPC

The RPC token has multiple applications within the RPC ecosystem, including facilitating borrowing and lending, staking, voting, participation in the RPC Launchpad for IDOs, and accessing benefits such as fee discounts, staking rewards, and crypto debit cards. RPC holders can also engage in the governance of the ecosystem by voting on project listings, partnerships, and developmental decisions.
